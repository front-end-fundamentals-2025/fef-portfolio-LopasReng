About the project of the Pencil Sharperner

This project was conducted in the spring of 2023 at my final year in Gothenburg Technical Gymnasium (GTG). In the course of Industrial design we were assigned the task to design a pencil sharperner, inspired by an established company. I cose to work with Apple as my inspirational company for my sharperner.
